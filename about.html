The idea is simple:

We match volunteers with amazing organizations for meaningful projects.

Basic idea:

  • As a volunteer: find projects you're interested in with as much commitment you're comfortable with (usually around 3 hours).
  • As an organization: list your project, and find amazing talent to help you out.
  • Let us do the hard work. We vet and match based on set critieria, including interests, profiles, and past accomplishments.
